drawDiagnosticPlot()¶

Draw the 4 usual diagnostic plots.

Returns:
gridGridLayout
Returns a grid of 4 graphs:

  • the QQ-plot,

  • the PP-plot,

  • the return level graph (with confidence lines),

  • the density graph.

Notes
