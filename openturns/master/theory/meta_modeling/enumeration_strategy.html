Chaos basis enumeration strategiesÂ¶

The functional chaos expansion allows one to obtain an explicit representation of the random response of the model under consideration. More precisely, the response is cast as a converging series featuring an orthonormal basis. For computational purpose, it is necessary though to retain a finite number of terms by truncating the expansion. First of all, a specific strategy for enumerating the infinite PC series has to be defined. This is the scope of the current section.
