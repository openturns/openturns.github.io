Gaussian calibration¶

Introduction¶

In this page, we present the method used in the GaussianLinearCalibration and GaussianNonLinearCalibration classes. In this case, simplifications occur which lead to an efficient algorithm that requires fewer model evaluations compared to Monte-Carlo Markov Chains (MCMC).
