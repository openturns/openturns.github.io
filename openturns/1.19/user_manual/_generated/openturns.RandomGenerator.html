RandomGenerator¶

class RandomGenerator(*args, **kwargs)¶

Uniform random generator.

Refer to Uniform Random Generator.

The random generator of uniform(0,1) samples is based on the DSFTM (Double precision SIMD oriented Fast Mersenne Twister) algorithm.

Parameters

Notes

This method fixes a particular state of the random generator algorithm thanks to the seed . The seed of the random generator is automatically initialized to 0 when a session is launched.
