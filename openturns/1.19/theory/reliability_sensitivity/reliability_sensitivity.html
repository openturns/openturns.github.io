Reliability, sensitivity¶

Sensitivity analysis methods can be used to analyze the respective importance of each uncertainty source with respect to a probabilistic criterion. This covers ranking methods for probabilistic criterion on central dispersion (expectation and variance) and probability of exceeding a threshold / failure probability.
