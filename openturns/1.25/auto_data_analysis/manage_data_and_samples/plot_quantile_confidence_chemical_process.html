In the example, we consider the quantile of level , with a confidence level of (see example 5.7 p. 85).

alpha = 0.1
beta = 0.95
algo = otexp.QuantileConfidence(alpha, beta)

Estimate bilateral rank. Indices are given in the integer interval whereas the book gives them in .

n = len(sample)
l, u = algo.computeBilateralRank(n)
print(f"l={l} u={u}")

l=3 u=15

Estimate the bilateral confidence interval of the 0.1 quantile from the order statistics:

..math:

\Prob{X_{(k_1)} \leq x_{\alpha} \leq X_{(k_2)}} \geq \beta

ci = algo.computeBilateralConfidenceInterval(sample)
print(f"ci={ci}")

ci=[2.24, 4.04]
